The following files are firmware for the Waveshare ESP32-S3-POE-ETH-8DI-8DO,
an industrial controller board featuring an ESP32-S3 with 16MB flash, 8MB
octal PSRAM, W5500 SPI ethernet with PoE, 8 optocoupler-isolated digital
inputs, 8 optocoupler-isolated digital outputs (via TCA9554PWR I2C IO
expander), RS485, CAN bus, SD card, PCF85063 RTC, RGB LED, and buzzer.
